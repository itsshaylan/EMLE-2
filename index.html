WHO WE ARE

It’s simple. We are a guilt-free online multi-brand retailer focused on unique and conscious brands. We don’t have all the answers about conscious living, hell, we never will but hopefully along our journey we can help you fill your closet with pieces made with the planet and it’s people in mind.

OUR CONSCIOUS SCALE

Four core pillars each including important issues we believe helps encompass a conscious closet. Put simply; it is a guideline we use to ensure what we value is represented by every brand we choose to carry.

WE ARE

  • Honest

  • Conscious

  • Friendly

  • Fun

  • High-Quality

  • Inclusive

WE ARE NOT

  • Phony

  • Green Washing

  • Fast Fashion

  • Cheap

  • Unattainable

  • Exclusive

When I began dreaming up EMLE, I wanted to make conscious living cool, accessible and easy for others. You can still look good and give a sh*t about our planet. The fashion industry gets a bad rep for being harmful in many aspects, but there is a way to shop unique fashion without feeling guilty or questioning the brand’s ethics. I always came back to providing a space where anyone and everyone could shop consciously without having to sacrifice their style. Now, here we are and EMLE is coming to life.
